Projects: The Best of the Best

A Record of the Unexplained Anomalies at the Museum of Pop Culture (May 2016-Present)

Description:

This solo project is my pride and joy. Formerly known as Pop Culture: Doctor Who (2016-August 2020), this game is an anime-inspired, interactive, visual novel
with the Doctor Who universe at the center of its plot. In addition to immersive storytelling, players will be put to the test by passing 33 challenging levels.

How it is Being Created:

  • This game is being programmed with the Unity game engine in addition with C# Scripts.
  • Assets such as voice lines, music, and art are being supplied by indpendent creators I have decided to collaborate with

Goal of this Project:

In addition to showing off my design, creative, and programming skills to potential employers, my goal is to give a unique gameplay
experience to everyone, fans and newcomers alike!
